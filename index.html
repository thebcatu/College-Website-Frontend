BSc-CSIT

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc-CSIT) is a four-year, eight-semester program affiliated with Tribhuvan University. The program focuses on comprehensive knowledge of computer science theory, programming, and modern IT applications.

BCA

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a four-year undergraduate degree program designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer applications and software development, preparing them for Nepal's growing IT industry.

BIM

Bachelor of Information Management (BIM) is a four-year program that blends IT knowledge with management skills. This program prepares students for roles that require both technical expertise and business acumen in Nepal's expanding digital economy.
